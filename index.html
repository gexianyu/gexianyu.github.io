Biography

From 2009 to 2013, I studied in Changchun University, Changchun, China, and received B.S. Degree in Information and Computing Science; From 2013 to 2016, I studied in Hefei University of Technology, Hefei, China, and received M.S. Degree in Computational Mathematics，my supervisor is Li Zhang; From 2017 to 2022, I studied in Hefei University of Technology, and received PH.D. Degree in Computer Software and Theory, my supervisor is Jieqing Tan; Since August 2022, I began to work in School of Electronic and Information Engineering from Anhui Jianzhu University. My current research interests include image deblurring and enhancement.
